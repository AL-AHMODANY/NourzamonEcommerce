Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?
Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days. International orders take 7-14 business days depending on location.

Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping rates vary by location and will be calculated at checkout.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

Yes, all our products are sourced from halal-certified suppliers. We ensure strict adherence to Islamic guidelines.
